* Game overview:
    * Get to top of mountain
        * Gets rockier (both falling rocks and random boulders around)
        * More enemies, different kinds, more powerful as you ascend
        * Random items
        * Two shops
            * Standard stuff at the shops
    * Randomly generated world:
        * Caves (these pop to a different screen)
               * Also smooth scrolling
               * Smaller than overworld
               * Miniboss + special weapon drop
        * Streams
            * They heal you
    * Defeat dragon at top of mountain
